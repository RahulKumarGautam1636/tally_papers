tally erp 9 comes with different access modes of accounts which we can use to set different passwords and usernames to give
different level of access to the company accounts. to use it press alt+f3 and go to security control --- users and passwords. now we can
set different usernames and passwords for different level of access. configure, save and now close company and and then open it with
the new username and password that you saved just few moments ago. you will have restricted controls to the company according to 
your settings.
